Our Mission

Bridging the gap between ambition and action.

Finding meaningful extracurricular opportunities shouldn't feel like a scavenger hunt. We bring high-quality programs, awards, scholarships, and resources into one supportive hub, so students can explore their passions and take action with confidence.

Whether you want to lead, volunteer, earn awards and grants, or start something of your own, CauseConnects is here to help you succeed.

Who can use Cause Connect Hub?
+
Cause Connect Hub is built for middle school and high school students who want to explore their passions, discover extracurricular opportunities, apply for awards and scholarships, and start their own initiatives.
Is there a cost involved?
+
Nope! Cause Connect Hub is 100% free for students. Access to opportunities should never be a barrier.
What kinds of opportunities can I find?
+
You can explore youth-led initiatives, volunteer roles, leadership opportunities, competitions, awards, scholarships, and resources to start your own extracurriculars.
Do I need prior experience to join opportunities?
+
Not at all. Cause Connect Hub is built for students at any stage, whether you're just starting out or already deeply involved.
How do I list my organization?
+
Click "Add Your Project" and fill out the form including information such as what you do and what your looking for. After about a week a confirmation email will be sent out!
